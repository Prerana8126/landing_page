üß† How the Game Works

Cricket is played between two teams of eleven players. One team bats to score runs, while the other bowls and fields to restrict runs and dismiss batters. Each team gets a turn to bat and bowl, and the team with the higher score wins.

The game is played in overs ‚Äî sets of 6 legal balls bowled by one player. Batters aim to score runs, while bowlers and fielders aim to get them out. The match has formats like Test, ODI, and T20, each demanding unique strategies and pacing.

üèè Cricket Formats Explained

Cricket is played in three main formats:

  • Test: Played over 5 days, focusing on patience, technique, and endurance.
  • ODI: One Day Internationals with 50 overs per side. Strategic and balanced format.
  • T20: 20 overs per side, fast-paced and designed for entertainment.

Understanding formats helps you analyze players, match conditions, and team strategies more deeply.
